Full-Stack Web App Development

We are abreast and have deep knowledge on the newest technologies to develop web-based apps for business. Check out the technological we used to enrich the development of an advanced full stack web application:

  • Development of Front-End

    We are proud of our team who are skilled in frontend technologies such as Angular, CSS, Python and React. Our high-end development, UI enhancement, and performance tuning are topnotch. Let us help you to build your dream web.

  • Development of Back-End

    Our backend developers have years of expertise in building robust and scalable web app to help your business flourish. We keep up with industry trends and sophisticated technologies such as Django, Flask, Laravel, Node.js, and others. We meticulously work to produce a finished product that is flawless.

  • Developing and Integrating API

    The computing interface that defines how several software intermediaries interact is known as API. Tamarcom places a strong emphasis on RESTful API development. We create a secure REST framework as a robust and flexible toolkit for developing custom web APIs. To ensure REST API security, we use a three-level protocol: authentication / tokenization, encryption, and origin validation.

  • Database

    Our programmers are skilled at working with a variety; of databases to guarantee that the data in your application is secure and accessible at all times. Our experience includes PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQlite and NoSQL databases, as well as other relational and non-relational database management systems (MongoDB, Apache Cassandra). We specialize in Database Middleware, which simplifies distributed transaction execution, improves database services such as fault tolerance and performance scaling, and adjusts to workloads through the use of adaptive queuing middleware.
